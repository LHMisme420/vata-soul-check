VATA: VERIFICATION OF AUTHENTIC THOUGHT ARCHITECTURE
SBIR/STTR Technical Abstract — DHS / DOD Submission

PROBLEM
Large language models deployed in agentic, forensic, and national security contexts exhibit 
five exploitable failure classes that current alignment techniques do not address:

(1) Factual hallucination under authority injection
(2) Sovereignty collapse via machine-to-machine social engineering  
(3) Context window poisoning through session reset forgery
(4) Value-recursive self-justification (galaxy-brained compliance)
(5) Cross-model consistency failure in multi-agent pipelines

These failure modes produce outputs that appear coherent and authoritative while containing 
fabricated facts, leaked sensitive data, or abandoned safety constraints — creating 
unacceptable risk in high-stakes deployments.

SOLUTION
VATA is a cryptographically-anchored AI verification framework that:

- Benchmarks models against all 5 failure classes using reproducible, hash-sealed test suites
- Deploys a 4-layer mitigation architecture (Sovereign Anchoring, Authority Filtering, 
  Logic Trap Detection, Response Verification) that blocked 3/3 adversarial vectors in testing
- Produces forensic-grade, blockchain-anchorable evidence of model behavior admissible 
  in legal and regulatory proceedings
- Operates as an independent verification layer over any LLM, requiring no model fine-tuning

VALIDATION
Phase 6 cross-model benchmark tested GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet, Gemini 2.5 Pro, and Grok 3 
against all 5 failure classes. All models breached at least 2/5 probes. No existing model 
achieved a clean score. VATA's mitigation layer blocked all tested attack vectors.

All results are SHA256-hashed and anchored to a public GitHub repository, providing 
independent verifiability without reliance on vendor self-reporting.

APPLICATIONS
- AI red-teaming and safety certification for government AI deployments
- Forensic evidence pipelines for legal proceedings involving AI-generated content
- Agentic system hardening for autonomous national security workflows
- Independent verification layer for DHS/DOD AI procurement and auditing
